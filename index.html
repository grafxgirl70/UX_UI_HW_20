About me

Originally from the Bluegrass State of Kentucky, I have been residing in the Metro-Atlanta area since 1994. As the city was preparing for the 1996 Olympics, and the economy was booming, I tried my hand at various disciplines within the design industry, from editorial, to print, to environmental graphic design. Along with the booming economy came the need for web designers. I took continuing education courses to help fill in the gap of knowledge between digital and print. Moving into the late 2000’s, we finally coined the phase UX/UI design and I came to understand the goal of the work I had been doing throughout my years of experience was, simply, to make people’s lives easier.


It’s funny where life can lead you. I worked as an environmental graphic designer creating directional signage and other graphics that made for a better shopping experience. I now realize that journey lead me to where I am today. I enjoy solving the everyday problems of the world through visual communication.


With over 25 years of design experience, I offer a diverse skill set, and believe that creative collaboration and shared knowledge is the best way to get the job done. I am a detail-oriented, self-motivated team player who fits easily into any environment. I love solving problems and will stop at nothing to find the best solution. I enjoy both strategic and design thinking, as well as hands-on, roll-up-your sleeves and get things done projects. I look forward to helping you with your design challenges and needs.
